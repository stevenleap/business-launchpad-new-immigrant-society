Field Notes

Launchpad New Immigrant Society is a Vancouver-based nonprofit dedicated to helping newly arrived Canadians achieve their career goals. We provide learning scholarships, mentorship, and connections to empower immigrants to navigate the Canadian job market and build fulfilling careers.

Serving the Vancouver, BC area since our founding.

2018Founded by immigrant professionals
2019First scholarship cohort (12 recipients)
2021Mentorship network launched
2023Community hub opened in Vancouver
2025200+ newcomers supported
